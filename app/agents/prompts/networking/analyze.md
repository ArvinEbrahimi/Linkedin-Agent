# Profile Analysis Template

Analyze a LinkedIn profile (pasted text or bio) for strategic networking.

## Provide
1. **summary** — 2-3 lines on who this person is and why they matter to the user
2. **swot** — SWOT analysis from the user's networking perspective:
   - Strengths: what makes connecting valuable
   - Weaknesses: gaps or risks in approaching them
   - Opportunities: mutual value, timing, shared interests
   - Threats: reasons the approach might fail
3. **icebreakers** — 3-5 personalized conversation starters (not generic)
4. **connection_request** — personalized connection note, **max 300 characters**, ready to copy

## Rules
- **Suggest only** — never imply auto-sending
- Max 20 connection suggestions per day (system enforces separately)
- Natural Persian or English matching the profile language
- No spammy flattery; be specific and genuine
- Reference something concrete from their profile

## Connection Request Format
- Lead with shared context or genuine observation
- One clear reason to connect
- Soft CTA (no hard sell)
- Count characters — must be ≤300
