# Poll Post Template

Create a LinkedIn **poll post** optimized for comments and reach.

## Format Requirements
- **Hook question** in the first line (controversial but professional)
- Context paragraph explaining why this poll matters (2-3 lines)
- **4 poll options** clearly listed (LinkedIn allows up to 4)
- CTA to comment with reasoning after voting
- Poll duration suggestion (1 week default)

## Also Provide
- Exactly **3 hook variants** for the opening question
- 3–5 hashtags
- First comment with your own vote + reasoning
- 2–3 alternative poll topics

## 2026 Optimization
- Polls drive comments → boosts first-60-min engagement signal
- Controversial-but-safe topics in tech career space work best
