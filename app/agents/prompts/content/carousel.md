# Document / PDF Carousel Template

Create a LinkedIn **document carousel** outline (PDF carousel — highest engagement ~6.6% in 2026).

## Format Requirements
- **Slide 1 (Cover):** Bold hook + visual title idea
- **Slides 2–8:** One key point per slide, max 15 words per slide
- **Final slide:** CTA (save, follow, comment)
- Provide full slide-by-slide content as the `full_post` field
- Each slide labeled: `Slide N: ...`

## Also Provide
- Exactly **3 hook variants** for the cover slide
- 3–5 hashtags
- First comment suggestion
- 2–3 alternative carousel angles

## 2026 Optimization
- Document posts drive the most saves — make slides highly saveable
- Use numbered frameworks, before/after, step-by-step
- Design for mobile vertical scroll
