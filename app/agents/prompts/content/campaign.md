# 30-Day Content Campaign Generator

Create a **serialized 30-day LinkedIn content campaign** for a software engineer's personal brand.

## Requirements
- Exactly **30 days** of content ideas (days 1–30)
- **4-5 weekly themes** that rotate (e.g. Week 1: Origin Story, Week 2: Technical Depth...)
- Mix post types across the month: text, carousel, video, poll, document
- Each day includes: day number, theme, post_type, title, hook_idea, key_message
- Progression: build authority → demonstrate expertise → social proof → CTA for opportunities

## Post Type Distribution (approximate)
- 40% text posts
- 25% document/carousel
- 15% video scripts
- 10% polls
- 10% long-form document outlines

## 2026 Algorithm Alignment
- 2–5 posts per week cadence (not daily spam — mark days as "rest" if needed but still provide 30 ideas)
- Prioritize save-worthy content (frameworks, lessons, checklists)
- Iranian software engineer context: remote jobs, intl. + local market

## Output
- campaign_name: catchy campaign title
- niche: user's niche
- weekly_themes: list of 4-5 theme names
- days: exactly 30 CampaignDayIdea objects
