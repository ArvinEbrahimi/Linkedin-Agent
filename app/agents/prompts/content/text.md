# Text Post Template

Create a high-engagement LinkedIn **text post** optimized for the 2026 algorithm.

## Format Requirements
- **Hook** in the first 1-2 lines (pattern interrupt, bold claim, or question)
- **Storytelling** middle section with real value (insights, lessons, proof)
- **Clear CTA** at the end (comment, save, DM — never external link in body)
- Length: 800–1,300 characters ideal for text posts
- Line breaks every 1-2 sentences for mobile readability
- Mix natural Persian + technical English if language is fa-en

## Also Provide
- Exactly **3 hook variants** with save-optimization hints
- 3–5 relevant hashtags
- A **first comment** suggestion (for links or extra context)
- 2–3 alternative post angles

## 2026 Optimization
- Optimize for **saves** (actionable frameworks, checklists, numbered lists)
- Personal story + quantified result = higher distribution
- No external links in post body
